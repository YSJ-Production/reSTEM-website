    Activities

    Hubs have the flexibility to conduct any STEM-related activities, though the focus is expected to be on research. The following activities are directly supported by the YSJ:

    • Exploration module: Learn about prospects in research and STEM
      • reSTEM Safari, a series of introductory sessions (seminars, activities etc) to introduce members to different fields of STEM like Artificial Intelligence, neuroscience, Bioinformatics, etc in collaboration with specialising organisations.
      • reSTEM Compass, a series of sessions on career and academic opportunities like science fairs, research programs, entrepreneurship, international university admissions, olympiads etc. in collaboration with specialising organizations.
    • Foundation module: Learn skills for research
      • Research 101, research skill building workshops for conducting research, writing research papers and participating in science fairs.
      • reSTEM Kaleidoscope- Talks by experienced scientists and student researchers about their experience.
    • Immersion module: Learn by performing research
      • Members will get access to an exclusive, curated database of guided science projects to learn research skills hands-on without having to conduct an original project.
      • reSTEM Challenge: Certificate for completing original research project and writing research paper.
      • Publish in the Young Scientists Journal.
      • Mentorship to selected original research projects.
      • Recognition for best projects.
    • Hub Journal: Learn to write about research and STEM
      • Produce local editions of the YSJournal for your community.
      • Award and recognition for best Hub Journal.
      • Training in writing and editing scientific articles and research papers.
      • Publication in the Young Scientists Journal for best articles.
      • May be featured in school magazines etc.
